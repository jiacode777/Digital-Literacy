Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
Do’s

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, bank details, or password with anyone.
Always verify the sender before approving any UPI payment request.
Enable transaction alerts and set a low daily UPI limit in your bank app.
Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable 2-Factor Authentication.
Install apps only from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
Think before you click – hover over links to check the real URL before opening.
Keep your phone, banking apps, and operating system updated regularly.
Report any suspicious call, message, or transaction immediately.

Reporting Channels

National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930 (24×7)

Extra Financial / UPI Safety Tips
9. Never click on unknown payment links received via WhatsApp, SMS, or email.
10. Double-check the UPI ID, name, and amount before every transaction.
